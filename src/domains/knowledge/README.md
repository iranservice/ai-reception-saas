# Knowledge Domain

**Owner:** Knowledge module
**Responsibility:** Knowledge bases, entries (FAQ, menu, business data), versioning foundation.

## Owns

- Knowledge base definitions
- Knowledge entries (FAQ, menu items, services)
- Entry versioning
- Knowledge search and retrieval

## Dependencies

- Identity, Tenancy

## Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Do NOT put AI inference here — that belongs in **AI Runtime**
- ❌ Do NOT put prompt templates here — that belongs in **AI Config**
